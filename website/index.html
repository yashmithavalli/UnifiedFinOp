Key Features

Everything you need to gain complete control over your organization's financial operations.

Centralized Financial Dashboard
A single pane of glass for all financial data. See every rupee flowing across departments, projects, and vendors instantly.
Department-wise Expense Tracking
Track spending by department with granular breakdowns. Identify budget overruns before they become problems.
Project-Level Cost Attribution
Allocate costs to specific projects and initiatives. Understand the true cost of every deliverable.
Automated Policy Enforcement
Define spending policies and let the platform enforce them automatically, flagging violations in real time.
Vendor Cost Analysis
Compare vendor pricing, track contract terms, and identify cost-saving opportunities across your supplier base.
Real-Time Financial Visibility
Live spending feeds, instant budget alerts, and dynamic dashboards that update as transactions happen.
